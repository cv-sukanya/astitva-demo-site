Health

Health is critical to human welfare and human productivity. Conscientious efforts have been made to promote and create accessibility to affordable and quality healthcare services at the taluka level, which is not available even in district headquarters. This initiative has impacted and helped people not only in the Shirpur Taluka but several other talukas near Shirpur.

Primary health

With the help of community leaders, and qualified medical professionals, well-equipped primary health centers were established to provide primary health facilities. Safe and secure medical aid can go a long way in community advancement. Recognizing this fact, several initiatives have helped treat 17000 people from 95 different villages, at none to negligible costs. Over and above this, about 350 medical camps are driven for check-ups and treatments of the people of 71 villages. These camps include eye camps, health check-up, polio diagnosis, camps related to kidney, diabetes, asthma; camps with experts, such as pediatricians, etc. Special attention is given to reproductive health. The health awareness camps educate the locals about the “dos” and “don’ts” of health.

The Gift of Vision

Eye camps have been set up periodically, which has bestowed sight, at negligible cost, to the local people in need. Aid has been made available, in the form of medicines, glasses, etc., for free or at minor costs. Moreover, eye care has been provided to very many cataract patients, treating all their concerns. Several cataract patients have been successfully operated on, and now enjoy clear sight.

Eye Camps

Moreover, eye care has been provided to very many cataract patients. Numerous eye camps are set up to furnish free aid and treatment for eye concerns. 35 cataract patients have been successfully operated on, and now enjoy the gift of clear sight.

COVID-19 Relief

The challenges thrown by the COVID-19 pandemic were met head on by provision of remdesivir at rupees 750, wherein the same drug was sold for as high as 50000 in the open market. Oxygen plants were set up to provide oxygen to critical patients. The people were encouraged, and facilities set up, for vaccinations. Taking things one step further, Astitva has distributed ration and masks to the population, in times of need. Thus far, 30000 and more families have been aided with medicines and vaccination, as well as other relief measures.
